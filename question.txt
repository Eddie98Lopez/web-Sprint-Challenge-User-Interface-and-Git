Name two big differences between display: block; and display: inline;.
A: display:block will unless otherwise specified have a width that fills the window.
    and have an automatic linebreak. Whereas display:inline will not have a linebreak 
    and display side by side with other elements. 
    so i guess the 2 differnces would be its default width and the default linebreak properties

A: it allows a team to make multiple edits by individual members and keep track of
    those edits without potentially breaking the source code. And in the event that
